Founder: Abiola Johnson Awowole (JAOYEM).

Also, he does his work with the fear of God, and is a Writer, full of Innovative and constructive ideas, with Classic Write-ups. He is popularly known as JAOYEM.

Our Motto
Building an Excellent mind-sets in the Children and Continously Improving on them so as to form a Cluster of EXCELLENCE...!

The School
De'Excellence group of school was established on April 2021.
De'Excellence Group of School is a school full of Dynamic ideas put in place to foster academic Excellence in our students, coupled with technical and Scientific skills in order to help in certain aspect of societal developments through the implementation of both Modern education and acustomed-way of learning. De'Excellence is established to help make your Child(ren) enjoy quality education.

Our Students
In De'Excellence group of school, we put our Students first by giving them the best in everything. We're having the best Students and we still look forward to admiting more Students.
